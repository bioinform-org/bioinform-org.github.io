{{ "About" }}

Bioinformatika (Bioinform) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) registered in Montenegro. We are focused on providing free educational contents in bioinformatics and science in general. We offer guidance on Erasmus+ opportunities and bioinformatics career paths to young students. In addition, we are dedicated to raising awareness of the position of women in science and the protection of the environment.

Our Youth Goals

Youth Goals were developed through a consultation with young people from all over Europe and therefore reflect the views of European youth. These are the Youth Goals that we share:

Quality Learning

Ensure equal access to quality education.

Support the development of interpersonal and intercultural skills.

Implement more personalized and participative teaching methods.

Youth Organisations & European Programmes

Increase visibility and accessibility of European youth programs and youth organization by providing quality information to potential participants.

Equality of All Genders

Ensure equal access to education for all genders.

Raise awareness of gender-based inequality and discrimination.

Eliminate stereotypical gender roles.

Sustainable Green Europe

Ensure that young people know the effect of their actions on the environment.

Empower young people to act as agents of change for environmental and sustainable development.
