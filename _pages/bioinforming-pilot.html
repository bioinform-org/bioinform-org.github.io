{{ "BioINForming - Pilot" }}

Participants: 6 high school students (5 in-person and 1 online)

Trainers: Katarina Elez and Marco Anteghini

Program

1. Introduction
  • Introduction to bioinformatics
  • Basics of molecular biology
  • Basics of Unix OS
2. Molecular sequence analysis
  • Next Generation Sequencing (NGS)
  • Pairwise sequence alignment
  • Sequence database search
  • Multiple sequence alignment
3. Molecular phylogenetics
  • Basics of phylogenetics
  • Phylogenetic tree construction

* Career and Erasmus+ opportunities


4. Protein structure analysis
  • Structure determination
  • Basics of protein structure
  • Protein structure prediction
  • Protein structure visualization, comparison and classification
5. Protein-ligand interactions
  • Basics of intermolecular interactions
  • Protein-ligand docking
  • Virtual screening for drug design
