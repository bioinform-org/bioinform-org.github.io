Bioinformatics

Bioinformatics is an emerging science that focuses on the storage, retrieval and analysis of biological data.

It is a highly interdisciplinary field that involves natural sciences (biology, chemistry and physics), but also computer science and mathematics / statistics.

About

Bioinformatika (Bioinform) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) registered in Montenegro. We are focused on providing free educational content in bioinformatics and science in general. We offer guidance on bioinformatics career paths to young students. In addition, we are dedicated to raising awareness of the position of women in science.

We share the following Youth Goals:

Quality Learning

Guarantee equal access to quality education.

Create and implement learner-centered methods.

Support the development of critical and analytical thinking, as well as interpersonal skills.

Equality of All Genders

Ensure that all genders have equal access to education.

Raise awareness of gender-based inequality and discrimination.

Empower girls and women through technology.

Inclusive Societies

Ensure that all marginalised people have equal access to education.

Address all the dimensions of inclusion.

Youth Organisations & European Programmes

Increase visibility and accessibility of European youth programs and youth organization by providing quality information.
